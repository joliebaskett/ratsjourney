After searching worlds far and wide the rat could not find the Kumquat of Truth. The rat had changed much on their journey, returning home different than those who knew him remembered.

When he told the village degu that they had failed in their mission, the degu told the rat that there really was no Kumquat of Truth.

The degu said that the Kumquat of Truth lies within us all, and that the journey of finding it is the journey of finding ourselves.
